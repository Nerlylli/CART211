Despite appearing to be a free platform, Spotify is a music streaming service and app that only works with a subscription. In fact, their premium plans includes ad-free music listening, improved audio quality, the ability to use the app while traveling overseas, and even the ability for many users to use a single subscription.

This pressures users to pay a monthly membership in order to use one of their Premium plan, as the free version makes the application hardly functional.

The best way to think about how Spotify uses our data, is to analyze Spotify Wrapped and the information it gives us at the end of each year.

Spotify Wrapped allows Spotify users to see a collection of the data collected, which has the information on their usage of the service during the previous 12 months, summarizing the five musicians, songs, and musical genres most listened to by a user. It even calculates how much time has spent listening to music on the platform.
